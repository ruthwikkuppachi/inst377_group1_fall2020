About Us


What is our project?


Government budget allocation is a complicated process of deciding how much is spent and where. The purpose of this website will be to reduce the complexity of the process so that both the government can analyse its spending and the average person can understand where the government spends its money. In addition, this application will provide transparency for the Prince George’s County government’s budget distribution.


This is where the Government Spending Project (GSP) comes in. The goal of the GSP is to encourage people find other ways to increase the fund from different part of Prince George's County. Educate the public on their current status of their spending and which parts need more funds.
